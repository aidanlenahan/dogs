About Me

Hello, my name is Aidan Lenahan. I am a local high school senior honors student. Since 2021, I've walked over 40 dogs and have loved every moment of it. My genuine passion for dogs drives everything I do. I have experience with dogs of all sizes—from small breeds to dogs as heavy as 120 lbs—and have handled up to 4 dogs at a time.

Your dog's safety, happiness, and exercise are my top priorities. I treat every dog I walk like my own, like family.

I offer competitive pricing and a very flexible schedule to those in the Monmouth County, NJ Peninsula area with the most dedicated care.
